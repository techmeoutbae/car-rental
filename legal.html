Liability & Booking Disclosures

Clear Terms Protect The Business And Inform The Guest.

These disclosures clarify risk, damage responsibility, insurance choices, prohibited use, and consent requirements before any reservation can proceed.

Core Legal Themes

  • Assumption of risk for high-performance driving
  • Damage, towing, and misuse responsibility
  • Deposit authorization and incident review
  • Optional insurance or verified personal coverage

Assumption Of Risk

By booking, the renter acknowledges that exotic and high-performance vehicles involve elevated operational risk. The renter accepts responsibility for lawful, safe vehicle use.

Damage & Liability

The renter is financially responsible for damage, abuse, prohibited use, negligent operation, interior damage, unauthorized drivers, and expenses not covered by approved insurance.

Insurance Choice

Guests may purchase supplemental coverage offered by the company or provide proof of qualifying personal coverage for approval before release of the vehicle.

Security Deposit

A refundable deposit authorization is required for every booking and may be retained pending inspection, claims review, unpaid fees, or policy violations.

Prohibited Uses

  • No racing, drifting, track use, burnouts, or speed contests.
  • No off-road use, towing, rideshare operation, or unlawful conduct.
  • No operation by unapproved or unverified drivers.
  • No impairment by alcohol, drugs, or unsafe fatigue conditions.

Booking Disclaimers

  • Availability is not guaranteed until payment and verification are accepted.
  • Vehicle substitutions may occur in rare service or safety circumstances.
  • The company is not liable for consequential losses, missed events, or personal injury resulting from unauthorized or unsafe vehicle use.
  • Final legal wording should be reviewed by licensed counsel in the target operating jurisdiction before publication.

Proceed With Transparency

Booking requires active acceptance of these disclosures.

Guests actively confirm these disclosures during the reservation request so expectations stay clear before final confirmation.
